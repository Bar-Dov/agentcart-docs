# AgentCart Documentation

AgentCart makes your store discoverable and purchasable by AI agents — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Mode, and others that use the [Universal Commerce Protocol](https://github.com/Universal-Commerce-Protocol/ucp).

## What AgentCart does

1. **Scans** your store and scores product data readiness for AI agents
2. **Connects** your store to the UCP network via a hosted manifest endpoint
3. **Enriches** product descriptions with AI-optimized content agents can parse
4. **Analyzes** agent activity — which products are requested, conversion rates, platforms
