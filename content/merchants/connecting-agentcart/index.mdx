# Connecting AgentCart

Connect your store to make it discoverable and transactable by AI agents. AgentCart installs directly into your e-commerce platform — no code, no manual API configuration.

Once connected, AgentCart publishes a UCP manifest at `yourstore.com/.well-known/ucp` so agents can discover your catalog, pricing, and checkout flow automatically.
