# Connecting Shopify

The AgentCart Shopify app handles everything automatically — it installs the UCP manifest on your store with no manual API tokens or URL redirects required.

## Prerequisites

- A Shopify store with **admin access**
- An AgentCart account ([sign up](https://app.agentcart.io))

## Installation

### Step 1 — Install the AgentCart app

Open the AgentCart app on the Shopify App Store and click **Install**.

> The Shopify App Store listing will be linked here once the app is live.

Shopify will ask you to confirm the required permissions. AgentCart requests read access to your products, variants, and collections in order to build your agent-readable catalog.

### Step 2 — Connect to your AgentCart account

After installation, the app redirects you back to your AgentCart dashboard. Your store is automatically linked to your account.

If you are not yet signed in, you will be prompted to log in or create an account first.

### Step 3 — Verify the connection

In your AgentCart dashboard, go to **Stores** and confirm your store shows a **Connected** status. AgentCart will run an initial scan within a few minutes.

## What happens after connection

- Your store is discoverable at `yourstore.myshopify.com/.well-known/ucp`
- An initial scan runs automatically to generate your first AgentCart score
- Weekly auto-scans keep your catalog data fresh
