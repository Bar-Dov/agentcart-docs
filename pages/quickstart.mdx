# Quickstart — Shopify

Get your Shopify store live on the UCP network in under 5 minutes.

import { Steps } from 'nextra/components'

<Steps>

### Create an account

Sign up at [app.agentcart.io](https://app.agentcart.io) and log in.

### Run your first scan

1. Go to **Scans → New Scan**
2. Enter your Shopify store URL (e.g. `mystore.myshopify.com`)
3. Wait ~2 minutes for the scan to complete

The scan grades your store's AI readiness across product data, descriptions, structured data, and UCP compliance.

### Connect AgentCart

1. Go to **Stores → [your store] → Settings** or click **Connect AgentCart →** in the Overview tab
2. Follow the 3-step wizard:
   - **Step 1:** Enter your Shopify Storefront API token ([how to get it](https://shopify.dev/docs/api/usage/authentication#getting-started-with-authenticated-access))
   - **Step 2:** Add a URL redirect in Shopify Admin: `/.well-known/ucp` → the URL shown in the wizard
   - **Step 3:** Click **Verify** — AgentCart confirms agents can reach your manifest

### You're live

Your store is now discoverable via the UCP network. AI agents can:
- Search your catalog
- Add items to a checkout session
- Redirect buyers to complete purchase

Check **Agent Activity** to see incoming agent requests.

</Steps>
