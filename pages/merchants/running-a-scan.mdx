# Running a Scan

A scan evaluates your store's AI readiness. You can run scans manually or they run automatically weekly once you're connected.

## Manual scan

1. Go to **Scans** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Scan**
3. Enter your store URL (e.g. `mystore.myshopify.com` or `https://mystore.com`)
4. Click **Start Scan**

The scan takes 1–3 minutes depending on catalog size. You'll see the status update in real time: **Crawling → Scoring → Simulating → Complete**.

## What gets scanned

| Category | What's checked |
|----------|---------------|
| Product titles | Length, clarity, keyword relevance |
| Descriptions | Presence, length, AI parseability |
| Images | Presence per product |
| Pricing | Consistent currency, no missing prices |
| Structured data | JSON-LD schema markup on product pages |
| Checkout | Can agents initiate a purchase? |

## Scan history

All past scans are saved under **Scans**. Each row shows:
- Store URL and platform badge
- Status (Complete, Failed, Crawling, etc.)
- Raw score
- AgentCart score (shown when scan post-dates your connection)

## Automatic weekly scans

Once your store is connected, AgentCart rescans automatically every 7 days to keep scores and enriched data current.
