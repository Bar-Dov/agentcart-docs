# Connecting AgentCart

Connecting your store routes AI agents through AgentCart's UCP manifest, enabling catalog search and checkout.

## Prerequisites (Shopify)

- Shopify store with admin access
- A **Storefront API access token** with `unauthenticated_read_product_listings` scope ([Shopify guide](https://shopify.dev/docs/api/usage/authentication#getting-started-with-authenticated-access))

## Setup wizard

Go to **Stores → [your store] → Settings** and follow the 3 steps.

### Step 1 — Storefront token

Enter your Shopify Storefront API token. AgentCart uses this to:
- Resolve product and variant IDs when creating checkout sessions
- Retrieve real cart totals (subtotal, tax, total) from Shopify

### Step 2 — URL redirect

Add a URL redirect in your Shopify Admin:

1. Go to **Shopify Admin → Online Store → Navigation → URL Redirects**
2. Create a redirect:
   - **From:** `/.well-known/ucp`
   - **To:** The AgentCart manifest URL shown in the wizard (e.g. `https://app.agentcart.io/api/ucp/your-slug`)

This redirect tells AI agents where to find your UCP manifest.

### Step 3 — Verify

Click **Verify**. AgentCart fetches `https://yourstore.com/.well-known/ucp` and confirms it resolves to your manifest. If it fails, check the redirect is saved and published.

## After connection

- Your store status shows **Agentic Commerce live** (green dot)
- Weekly auto-scans begin
- The **Agent Activity**, **Optimized Data**, **Agent Playground**, and **Simulations** tabs become available
- The next completed scan will show your AgentCart score alongside the Raw score
